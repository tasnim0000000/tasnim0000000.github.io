I'm a Computer Science & Engineering undergraduate at Southeast University, Dhaka. My work lives at the intersection of visual design and frontend engineering — building interfaces that don't just look good, but feel natural to use.

My obsession with design started with a simple question: why do some products feel effortless while others feel painful? That question led me into Figma, into CSS, into component architecture — and I haven't looked back.

I believe the best frontend work is invisible — it serves the user so naturally that the technology disappears. That's what I build toward.
